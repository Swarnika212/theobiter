CLAT & Other Law Entrance Exams

The Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) is India’s premier national-level entrance examination for undergraduate law programs. It acts as the gateway to 24 National Law Universities (NLUs) and leading private law schools.

Exam Overview:

  • Mode: Offline (Pen & Paper)
  • Total Marks: 120
  • Duration: 2 Hours
  • Negative Marking: 0.25 per wrong answer
  • Eligibility: No upper age limit; minimum 45% in 10+2 for General/OBC, 40% for SC/ST
  • Seats: 3,000+ across NLUs (as of 2024)
